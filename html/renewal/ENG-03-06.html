Giving Leader

The Giving Leader which began since 2017 is the donation program for individuals who agreed to donate 1 million KRW per year. In April 2018, professor Jonghan Lim donated condolence money with his father’s name Hengdal Lim to someone without family as their funeral fund, and in December, baseball player, Ahsub Son provided baseball equipment worth 200 million KRW to other baseball players in Busan, so he became giving leader.
