Support for Basic Needs

The Community Chest of Korea delivers warmth to our neighbors who do not receive welfare benefits.
In particular, we help them to satisfy their basic needs and to emerge from poverty through living expense support, food sharing, and material support.

Support for seniors during the summer

Scorching heat in every summer in Korea threaten many seniors’ health. According to the Ministry of Health, 6 people were seniors among 11 people who died due to heat waves in 2017. Whereupon, Gyeongbuk senior welfare association and Community Chest of Korea made a goods support project called ‘Cool Cool summer kit for seniors’. The kit is composed of 15 kinds of goods like blanket, mos￾quito repellent, emergency kit, electric fly swatter, cool towel, chicken soup, and noodle, etc. Seniors really loved the kit because it included everything they needed. One se￾nior who got the blanket happily said, “I definitely will have a cool summer with the blanket.”
