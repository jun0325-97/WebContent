Philanthropy Experience Center where the culture of giving can be understood by various activities

'Fruit of Love Philanthropy Experience Center' a very special site for learning about giving located in the downtown Seoul. It has completed its refurbishment in December 2016 and reopened in April 2017 for regular op￾eration. The philanthropy experience center is a participatory place of learning on giving, which helps prechool children, elementary, middle, high school students, and even adults understnad the concept of giving through various games and activities including the maze of giving, culture of giving in traditions, disability experience and so on in pledging the practice of giving in their daily lives. In addition, it strives to make our community a better place in which everyone lives together with consideration for one another, by the spread of culture of giving.
