Giving Leaders Club

Various types of affinity groups such as alumni associations, fan clubs, family gatherings, are eligible for the Giving Leaders Club, when committing 10 million KRW over 3 years. In 2018, a number of affinity groups including enterprises' group NECUS and pro-golfer Hae Rym Kim’s fan club joined the program.
