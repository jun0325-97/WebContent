Annual Campaign with Hope

We celebrated the success of 2019 Annual Campaign with Hope on January 31, 2019. Since its kick-off event on November 20, 2018, we raised 418.1 billion KRW for 73 days, and as a result, the Thermometer of Love hit 101.8’C. We received many written messages full of encouragements and inspiration that citizens left at the Postbox with Hope, located at Thermometer of Love.
