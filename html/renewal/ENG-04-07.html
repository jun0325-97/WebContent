We see hope when we turn isolation into communication. The Community Chest of Korea weaves a social safety net and runs community projects building capacity of unprivileged populations. By partnering with local agencies, we pursue community engagements and the spirit of living united.

We share challenges even with different skin colors

Ms. Lee who was born from Korean father and Cambodian mother was hard to make friends because they discriminated her, so she had no confidence about it. But, after she participated in program “The Story about us”, she introduced Cambodian culture, and fruit etc., and she found her smile again. 20 youths participated in the project “the story about us’ in Seosaeng-myeon, Onyang-eup, Ulju-gun, it is media academy where it establishes to form friendship culture. Participants achieved ego-identity and accomplish￾ment while creating the video “We understand. We also did that before.” Also, they shared empathy with other partici￾pants so, they feel despite of different race it doesn’t mat￾ter.

Not ready to communicate with the world but a beautiful challenge

Although many institutions educate about right of the disabled, but its contents are not realistic or exclude violation of disabled rights. Uiwang handicapped welfare center made project to improve the education of the dis￾abled right and its awareness the disabled webtoon author training project “3 cuts to show me”, so the stories of the webtoon are about the life and experiences of the disabled. While processing the project, all participants shared con￾cerns about the problems of the disabled which everyone experiences. Moreover, the disabled could participate as assistant authors, so they accomplished the self-help. As a result, total 62 episodes were upload on the web by the dis￾abled who finished the education 30 times. Moreover, the disabled could actively express their own opinions, so their life changed from passive to active.
