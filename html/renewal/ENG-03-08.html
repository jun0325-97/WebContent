Honor Society

The Honor Society is a giving program for individuals who commit more than 100 million KRW. The members with diverse backgrounds give positive impact over our society. The Honor Society, began with 6 members in 2008, celebrated its 2000th membership in December 2018, by inducting Junghun Seo, CEO of General Bio and GCoop, into the Society.

Junghun Seo who joined the Honor Society as the 2,000th member

CCK welcomed the Honor Society’s 2000th mem￾ber. Junghun Seo, CEO of General Bio and GCoop, received the donor recognition plaque from Chairman Jongsuk Ye on December 31, 2018. He said “It is my honor to join this prestigious society. I believe that the honor is followed by actions with good will and hope that we can promote the philanthropy even more in the society.” He also said “As a person running my own social enterprise, I would like to im￾pact the society by contributing the spirit of new generation and social entrepreneurship.” The Honor Society celebrated its 2000th membership due to the rapid growth that happened after March 2013 when it recognized 100th membership.
