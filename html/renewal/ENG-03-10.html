Corporate Giving

Community Chest of Korea designs and proposes customized CSR projects reflecting corporate needs, and helps companies realize social value over economic value. Based on the optimal gift management system that was built for the past 20 years, CCK helps companies create opportunities of leading sustainability with social contribution.

Strong foundation to sustain a the warm-hearted society

Major corporations including Samsung Group (50 billion KRW), Hyundai Motor Group (25 billion KRW), SK Group (12 billion KRW), LG Group (12 billion KRW), POSCO Group (10 billion KRW), KB Financial Group (10 billion KRW), Shinhan Financial Group (10 billion KRW), Hana Financial Group (10 billion KRW), and Lotte Group (7 billion KRW) have hugely contributed to the society by making large gifts.
