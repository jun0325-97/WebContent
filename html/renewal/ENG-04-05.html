Support for Mental & Emotional Health

Gyeongnam child protection institution by sup￾porting of Community Chest of Korea operates customized protection program for child abuse and its victim child. The program trained as customized education for 5,785 people, who are parents of minor children or preschool children, so we tried to protect child abuse and to find victim child earlier. Also, we performed mental treatment 128 times for 8 victim children who did not have welfare benefits and consulted 2 times to protect re-abuse with 12 parents who abused their kids. So, we tried to reduce emotional problems of victim children and parents who abused their children and helped to build fellowship within families. Ms. Kim, who was abused but did not receive treatment, found emotional comfort and improved the relationship with her family.

You are not alone; we are here for you

The life social welfare institution performed the community program “Family” to reduce loneliness of se￾niors who live alone at permanent leasing apartments, and to reinforce their social network. 49% of seniors are living alone at home in the region of the center. So, their social network and communication are lacking in comparison with their deep loneliness. So, the institution made a chance on rooftop of the institution to communicate each other like cultivating farm and eating together. Participants said “we had no friends so felt depression from loneliness before, but now we are happy to have new friends.” One of them said, “I am really happy to make friends here.”
